The One Club

The one Club is Men's fitness club formed to help young men turn into responsible adults.
Our main activities are sporting events that help older men bond with the young men, as they teach them the values of both Team and individual sport. We also provide employment opportunities to young men through their mentors who are mostly succesful business or career men.


The activities we have include:

  • Football
  • Weight Training
  • Lawn Tennis
  • Running
  • Discussion Forums
