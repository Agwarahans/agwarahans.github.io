In my estimation a person is punish by his sins not for his sins. Sinning is something like missing a mark, targeting but not hitting the bull's eye. To hit the bull's eye, you need to sacrifice your today by preparing that tomorrow you will hit it. sacrificing is like bargaining with the future, doing what will make tomorrow more bearable. Often when you aim and miss and end up sinning, it is usually because your sacrifice like Cain's was not accepted; that your sacrifice wasn't good enough to help you hit the bull's eye. The important thing is to prepare again and this time make a better sacrifice and you will hit your aim.

Perhaps I also need to remind you that you cannot hit the target without aiming, to aim is akin to asking, knocking and seeking, if you are to get somewhere you are therefore required to aim at it.
